Kilimanjaro and Karibujami Host Valentine's Day Event at UJ

Written by: Luyanda Msiza Venue: Karibujami Ladies Residence Date: 18 September 2025

On the 14th of February, Karibujami Ladies Residence hosted a Valentine’s Day event for both Karibujami and Kilimanjaro residences at the University of Johannesburg, Auckland Park Kingsway Campus. This was one of the greatest events hosted by the mentors to warmly welcome the first-year students.

The event featured multiple fun and engaging games that brought students together in a relaxed and vibrant atmosphere. It created a space for first-years to connect, interact, and enjoy their new journey at varsity in a supportive and lively environment.

It was a memorable night filled with laughter, bonding, and good vibes — setting a positive tone for the rest of the academic year.
