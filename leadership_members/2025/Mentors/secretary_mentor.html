Simphiwe Ndala

Born in KwaNdebele, Simphiwe Ndala is a cultural activist, communicator and radio host passionate about storytelling and intercultural communications. Affectionately known as Kamanda Ice Spice, he serves Kili as Secretary Mentor. He is a PR and Communications student with interests of pursuing postgraduate studies in Intercultural Communication. Simphiwe also presents State Your Mind on Ikwekwezi FM, an SABC education program that tackles social, cultural and health issues among children. Through broadcasting, he uses media as a platform to inform, inspire and change. With his blend of academia, cultural advocacy and media experience, Simphiwe continues to shape narratives that preserve heritage while empowering the next generation of young leaders. His motto: Driven by purpose, defined by impact.
